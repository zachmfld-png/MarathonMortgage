Zach Mayfield (NMLS 1698485) has been originating residential mortgage loans since 2017, helping homebuyers and homeowners navigate one of life’s biggest financial decisions with clarity and confidence. After gaining experience at large, nationwide lenders, Zach saw firsthand how big-box mortgage companies often lack awareness of local markets and struggle to offer truly competitive pricing. That experience shaped his commitment to doing things differently.

Today, Zach focuses on delivering the best possible rates paired with unmatched service. He understands that every market is unique and every borrower’s situation deserves personal attention—not a one-size-fits-all approach. His goal is simple: provide transparent guidance, strategic loan options, and consistent communication from the initial application all the way through closing. Clients can count on continuous accessibility and direct contact throughout the entire process, ensuring no questions go unanswered and no details fall through the cracks.

Outside of mortgage lending, Zach brings the same discipline and endurance to his personal life. He is an ultra-marathon runner who thrives on long-distance challenges, a mindset that translates directly into his persistence and dedication to his clients. During the summers, he serves as a race director for Great Lakes Endurance in Michigan’s Upper Peninsula, helping organize endurance events that bring athletes and communities together.

Zach is married to his wonderful wife, Rachel, and shares his home with two greyhound dogs, Oscar and Bruce. Whether he’s guiding borrowers to the closing table or directing a race across the rugged terrain of the Upper Peninsula, Zach is driven by preparation, commitment, and follow-through. His clients value not only his industry knowledge and competitive loan options, but also the steady, reliable support he provides every step of the way.
